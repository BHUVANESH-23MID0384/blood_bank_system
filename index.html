    Donor Eligibility Criteria

    To be eligible to donate blood, you must:

    • Be in good health.
    • Be at least 18 years old.
    • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
    • Not have donated blood in the last 8-12 weeks.
    • Meet the minimum hemoglobin level requirements.

    Certain medical conditions, medications, and travel history may affect your eligibility. Please consult with a healthcare professional or blood bank staff for specific questions.
